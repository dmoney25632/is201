Rockets

A fascination with space exploration and rocket technology.

Why Rockets?

Rockets are some of the most impressive machines ever built. From the Saturn V to SpaceX's Falcon 9, the history of rocketry is a story of ambition and engineering. We are living through a new space race driven by both governments and private companies.

Types of Rockets

  1. Orbital Rockets
    • Reach orbit around Earth
    • Examples: Falcon 9, Atlas V
  2. Suborbital Rockets
    • Reach space but don't complete a full orbit
    • Examples: Blue Origin New Shepard
  3. Heavy Lift Vehicles
    • Carry very large payloads
    • Examples: Falcon Heavy, NASA SLS
