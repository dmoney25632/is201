🎮 Play: Alter Ego

A classic NES homebrew adventure game — 100% free and legal to play. Use your keyboard or the on-screen buttons below.

Controls
  • Arrow Keys — Move
  • Z — A Button
  • X — B Button
  • Enter — Start
  • Shift — Select
  • On-screen gamepad also available
About This Game

Alter Ego is a puzzle-platformer created by indie developer Shiru in 2010 as a free NES homebrew game. You control a character and their shadow simultaneously. It's been praised as one of the best NES homebrew games ever made. No copyright issues — this ROM is freely distributed by its creator.
